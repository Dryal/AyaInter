About Us

We are a team of experts in logistics and international trade with over 30 years of experience, ready to support your business towards global expansion.

We are a team of passionate experts in logistics and international trade with over 30 years of experience. Discover how our seasoned professionals can propel your business to the global stage.

With a strong presence in the strategic cities of Casablanca and Tangier, AYA Intercontinental is a leading authority in the logistics industry. Drawing on decades of experience and an unwavering commitment to operational excellence, our company is positioned as a reliable partner for all your logistics needs in North Africa.

At AYA Intercontinental, our highly skilled team of consultants, passionate about international trade law, bring a wealth of knowledge to each case. With diverse expertise in commercial litigation and customs, we ensure that our clients benefit from a collective set of skills and creative solutions to resolve their legal issues.

In addition, our dedicated team of logistics professionals, specially trained to meet the diverse needs of our clients, offer sophisticated customs solutions and advanced supply chain management. We are committed to being your trusted partner for all your logistics requirements in North Africa.
